Tanamera
500 UAH / 1000g

Arabica Indonesia Sumatra Tanamera - coffee from South-East Asia with original characteristics and unforgettable taste. It is grown on the alpine plantations of the legendary island of Sumatra at an altitude of 1-2 km above sea level. More precisely, the plantations are located on the northern part of the island.

Carefully harvested coffee fruits are "semi-dry" processing, which allows you to combine the advantages of wet and dry processing of beans. Thus, on the one hand, the strength and richness are preserved, and on the other hand, the complexity and depth of taste and aroma properties. The taste of this variety is bright, sweetish, bready, with chocolate and nutty notes and bitterness.

If you have not tasted coffee varieties from Southeast Asia, we suggest you start with Arabica Indonesia Sumatra Tanamera.
