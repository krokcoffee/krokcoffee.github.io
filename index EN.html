Our advantages

Freshly picked coffee from all over the world

The taste and aroma of a type of coffee directly depends on the place in which it is grown. The coffee that you can buy from us grows on sunny plantations: Brazil, Colombia, Guatemala, Peru, Ecuador, Jamaica, Ethiopia, Kenya, Uganda, India, Indonesia, Thailand and Vietnam. Also one of the main the advantage is that we only sell coffee from the current harvest.

Exotic varieties for true coffee connoisseurs

If you think that coffee can no longer surprise you with its taste, then try the most exotic varieties of this drink, such as Black Ivory and Kopi Luwak.

The unique and rich flavors of coffee with subtle fruity and caramel notes conquer gourmets from the first sip of this aromatic drink. Try it too!
