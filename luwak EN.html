Kopi Luwak

Coffee
Kopi Luwak
800 UAH / 100 g

Kopi Luwak is one of the most expensive coffee in the world. The main factor behind its high price is its unusual production method. This elite coffee is not made from a specific type of coffee, but from coffee beans Arabica that have been "processed" in the intestines of a small animal of the civet family - luwak.

The process of making coffee "Kopi Luwak" is that the luwaks eat the ripe fruits of the coffee tree, but they only digest the pulp surrounding the coffee beans, and the beans themselves pass through its digestive system, where they undergo fermentation under the influence of certain enzymes. Undigested coffee beans are then harvested by people, washed and dried in the sun.

Kopi Luwak has a balanced taste with bitterness and a pleasant chocolate-caramel flavor and a bright, rich aroma. Any coffee lover should try Kopi Luwak!
