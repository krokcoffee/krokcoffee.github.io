Laughing Man

Coffee
Laughing Man
350 UAH / 1000g

Arabica Ethiopia Jimma Laughing Man is a true coffee legend. After all, Ethiopia, or rather Yemen, which was under the rule of Ethiopia, is considered the country in which they first began to prepare and consume a coffee drink. Jimma is the largest coffee province in this country.

Arabica Ethiopia Jimma Laughing Man contains a fairly large amount of caffeine compared to other types of arabica. The taste of this type of coffee is rich, with a noticeable bitterness, slight sourness and thick wine aftertaste. The aroma is persistent, pronounced.

This variety is perfect for brewing Turkish coffee and for other oriental methods of preparing a coffee drink.
